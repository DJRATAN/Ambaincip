Frequently asked Questions

What types of scholarships are available for the Marketing Fellowship?
We offer four types of scholarships that could provide you with up to 40% waiver on the fellowship. These are tailored for different categories of applicants like entrepreneurs, operators, women representation, athletes. For details about the eligibility criteria and application process, please get in touch with our admissions team by completing your application.
Do I get adegree after completing this fellowship?
No. This is a fellowship that helps you acquire elite Marketing acumen. We do not believe in degrees. We follow a practical-pedagogy to give you hands-on experience. You do get a certificate at the end but the more significant takeaway would be your learnings, network and opportunities!
What is the eligibility to join the fellowship?
To put it out there bluntly, "We just need people who are hungry for success and disrupting." Our founding cohort consists of learners who have less than 3 years of work experience to operators who come with 20+ year of experience. They have 2 things in common - Either they want to build/scale a startup or become the top 1% marketing leader and crack leadership roles.
